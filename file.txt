Complexity (Big-O analysis) of Algorithms:
- NP-complete
- Traveling salesman & knapsack problem


Sorting:
- Bubble sort  -  Best: O(n)  Worst: O(n^2)
- Selection sort  -  O(n^2)
- Insertion sort  -  Best: O(n)  Worst: O(n^2)
- Merge Sort  -  O(n*log(n))
- Quicksort  -  Best: O(n*log(n))  Worst: O(n^2)


Data Structures:
- Arrays
- Hashtables & maps
- Singly LinkedLists & DLL
- Stacks
- Trees  -  O(n)  Average: O(logn) since we divide operation as we execute a method
- Queues/priority queues
- Graphs
